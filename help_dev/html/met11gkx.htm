PC Metronome v1.0 Help Sheet


Click the Tempo buttons to adjust the tempo up or down. You can keep the button pressed to quickly change the value by a large amount. The range is 30 to 300 Beats Per Minute.


You can also select a tempo by clicking the Tap button in rhythm.


Click the Volume buttons to adjust the volume up or down. The range is from 0 to 10. Make sure your computer volume control (usually located close to the clock in the system tray) is turned up.


Clicking in this area brings up a submenu. It allows you to set the beat subdivisions, change the sound, and play an 'A' tone for tuning. Checking the 'Stay on top' box will cause the metronome to remain the topmost window when it is open with other windows open on your computer.


Click the Close button to exit PC Metronome.
